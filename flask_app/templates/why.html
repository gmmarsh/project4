Why did we choose this topic for our project?


  • Product managers, marketing people and product engineers just to name a few all need to understand what customers think about their product.

  • One way to understand what customers think about a product is to analyze the reviews they leave.

  • Topic modeling can help us understand what the customer thinks about a product by analyzing the reviews they leave.

  • By understanding what the customer thinks about a product, companies can make better decisions about how to improve their product.

  • The challenge or gap is identifying themes or topics in an efficient manner.

  • Our project aims to address this challenge by using topic modeling to identify themes in a sample of amazon customer reviews in an efficient manner.
