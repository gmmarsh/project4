What is Topic Modeling?

  • a type of statistical model that is used to discover abstract topics within a collection of documents
  • a tool for organizing, understanding, and summarizing large collections of textual information
  • used to discover the hidden thematic structure in a large collection of documents
  • a form of unsupervised learning
  • source:https://levity.ai/blog/what-is-topic-modeling#:~:text=Topic%20modeling%20is%20a%20type,predefined%20tags%20or%20training%20data.

How does it work?

  • Topic modeling algorithms work in a two-step process:
  • First, the algorithm takes a collection of documents and identifies the words that are most likely to appear in each topic
  • Second, the algorithm assigns each document to a topic based on the words that appear in it
  • source:https://levity.ai/blog/what-is-topic-modeling#:~:text=Topic%20modeling%20is%20a%20type,predefined%20tags%20or%20training%20data.

What are some applications of Topic Modeling?

  • Document Clustering
  • Recommendation Systems
  • Information Retrieval
  • Content Analysis

What are some popular Topic Modeling algorithms?

  • Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA)
  • Latent Semantic Analysis (LSA)
  • Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF)
